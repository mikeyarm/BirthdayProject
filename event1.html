On my Birthday...
Raiders of the Lost Ark

Raiders of the Lost Ark is the iconic 1981 adventure film directed by Steven Spielberg and produced by George Lucas, marking the first Indiana Jones film. The story follows the legendary archaeologist Indiana Jones (played by Harrison Ford) as he races against Nazi agents to find the Ark of the Covenant, a powerful ancient artifact. It's a high-octane blend of action, humor, and suspense, with memorable set pieces like the famous boulder chase. When it hit theaters on June 12, 1981, Raiders quickly became a cultural phenomenon, setting the standard for adventure films and cementing Ford's role as one of cinema's greatest action heroes.
