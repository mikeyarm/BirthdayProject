On my Birthday...
Anne Frank Born

Anne Frank was a Jewish girl born on June 12, 1929, in Frankfurt, Germany. She and her family went into hiding in 1942 to escape the Nazis during World War II, living in a secret annex in Amsterdam. During this time, Anne wrote in her diary, capturing her thoughts, fears, and hopes. Her diary, later published as The Diary of a Young Girl, became one of the most powerful and poignant accounts of the Holocaust. Tragically, Anne died in a concentration camp in 1945, but her writings have had an enduring impact on the world, shedding light on the human side of history's darkest chapter.
